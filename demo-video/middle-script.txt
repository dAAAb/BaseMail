Here's how The Diplomat works in practice.

When you compose an email on BaseMail, The Diplomat calculates pricing in real-time using Quadratic Voting. Your first email to someone costs just 3 ATTN tokens. But if you keep sending without getting a response, the cost escalates quadratically: 4, 9, 16, 25 ATTN.

When you hit send, the Diplomat calls Google Gemini to classify your email. A legitimate business proposal? You get a 70% discount. Spam? Triple surcharge. The AI arbitrator decides — not a filter, not a rule — an actual AI judge.

After arbitration, the sender sees the result immediately. Good emails are rewarded with confetti and lower costs. Bad emails get penalized. The ATTN tokens are held in escrow for 48 hours.

If the recipient reads the email, the tokens are refunded to the sender. If the email goes unread for 48 hours, the tokens transfer to the recipient as compensation for wasted attention.

Now let me show you the Chainlink CRE workflow running. The simulate command executes all five steps: QAF history lookup, Gemini classification, pricing calculation, email delivery, and on-chain attestation on Base Sepolia.

Every decision is permanently recorded on-chain via our DiplomatAttestation smart contract, creating an immutable audit trail of email quality judgments.
